# some terminology and/or slang

`#` sh, hash, or (it's true name...) octothorpe\
`!` bang\
`*` splat or star\
`/` wack or slash\
`\` backwack or backslash\
`[]` brackets\
`{}` braces or curly brackets\
`-` hyphen or tack\
`.` dot\
`~` tildy\
`@` at\
`'` prime (arcminute)\
`"` double prime (arcsecond)
